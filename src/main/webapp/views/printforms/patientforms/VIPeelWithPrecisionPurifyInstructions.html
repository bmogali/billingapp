Day 1

Day 2

Apply the post peel cream 2-3 times a day. Apply the VI Derm SPF 50+ 30 minutes prior to going outdoors. Repeat every 2 hours if exposed to sunlight.

1 hour before bedtime at                                        cleanse the skin with VI Derm cleanser and rinse with cold water. Dry thoroughly and rub the 3rd towelette labeled #3 VOFGOROUSLY over the peeled area, including eyelids, but avoiding the eyes (Do not wash off).

Day 3

Your skin will begin to peel. Continue applying the post peel protectant 2-3 times a day and the VI Derm SPF 50+, 30 minutes prior to going outdoors.

DO NOT:

  1. DO NOT apply anything on the skin for the next 4 hours after peel application.
  2. DO NOT exercise for the next 72 hours to avoid blisters.
  3. DO NOT wash your skin with hot water to avoid premature peeling.
  4. DO NOT expose skin to direct sunlight, to avoid hyperpigmentation and melasma.
  5. DO NOT scratch or pick your skin during peeling phase to avoid scarring and pigmentation.

MUST DO:

  1. Apply SPF 50+ on your skin when exposed to sunlight.
  2. Wear a hat when exposed to sunlight.
  3. Apply VI Derm Post Peel Protectant 2-3 times daily until skin is completely healed.
  4. Note: If discomfort or reaction occurs, which is rare, wash your face with a mild cleanser and cold water. You may also take ibuprofen.
