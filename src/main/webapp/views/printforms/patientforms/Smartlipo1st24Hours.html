Congratulations you have successfully completed the Smartlipo procedure! You are now on your way to a more beautiful and confident YOU!

Please follow your recovery outline and DO NOT deviate! You have invested the money now invest the time to make it right.

1st 24 Hours

  • Drink plenty of fluids (recommended- Gatorade, POM juice, and plenty of water.) Please note you will be urinating quite a bit and this is normal. You have been injected with a liquid that needs to be eliminated from your body.
  • Take Advil for the pain as recommended on the bottle-every 6 hours. Take Benadryl if become itchy from foam.
  • The body suit has to remain on for 7 days – Yes no shower! Sponge bath and body sprays should work. You may also want to purchase an additional suit to swap out of.
  • You will be draining for 24 to 48 hours from the incisions from surgery, this is normal, so you will have to change pads frequently until dry.
  • You will have pads covering the holes; make sure to change them as needed. Once the pads are dry do not cover up the holes. Let them heal on their own. No bandages or creams on the holes.
  • You will be swollen and sore, this is normal. If there is major discoloration or abnormal bruising, please contact me immediately.
  • Make an appointment with me for a follow up within the next 2-3 weeks after the procedure.

Please note the following: Smartlipo results will continue to improve within the next 12 months. Make sure you gain NO more weight than your preoperative recorded weight. Weight gain may result in a change of body contouring.
