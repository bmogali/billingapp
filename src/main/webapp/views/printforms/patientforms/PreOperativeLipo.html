SMARTLIPO PRE-OPERATIVE INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Please read these pre- and post-operative instructions and the surgical consent carefully in advance of your surgical date. Call the office as needed to answer your questions in order to be fully prepared for your smartlipo procedure.
  2. Make sure your physician and the staff are aware of all medications you are currently taking (both prescription and over the counter and any prior medical conditions you may have).
  3. Please fill the prescription you were given. Take the medications as indicated.
                                                                                                                                                                                                      
  4. DO NOT TAKE ASPIRIN, VITAMINS, HERBS, FISH OIL, MOTRIN, IBUPROFEN or medications for 2 weeks prior to your surgery unless authorized by the surgeon.
  5. You may take Tylenol for pain if needed before surgery.
  6. Do not intake any alcohol the day before or day of surgery.
  7. Please bring a towel for your vehicle do to any leaking you may experience after surgery.
  8. Please note to the doctor if any changes in health status such as signs of fever, cold or infection that develop prior to surgery.
